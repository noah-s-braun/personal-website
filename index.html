Essex Industries

Modern Careers Page

A Manufacturing Company with a Culture to Brag About

Essex Industries makes critical parts for aircraft across the globe—which means they need the best of the best working for them. In order to attract top talent, they needed a robust careers page that showcased their welcoming, family-based culture.

Crafting a clear user experience

Essex’s previous careers page, while functional, lacked a clear information flow. I organized the already existing sections into an intuitive progression—sharing who Essex is as an employer (culture, values, mission) before showing page users how they might fit in the company (available roles, employee testimonials.)

The Interface-Lift

Created to appeal to serious military contractors, Essex’s brand is bare bones. However, I pushed the interface design in a (slightly) more fun direction for this page to give prospective employees an engaging experience featuring colorful and smooth animations.

Building the Page

Essex’s website is managed entirely with WordPress, which made writing custom code a challenge, but after some trial and error, I discovered how to incorporate the three web languages (HTML, CSS, and JavaScript) into the page. This allowed me to build the complex interactions I imagined during the design stage.
