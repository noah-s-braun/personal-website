Designing a tech-forward agency website

GBG, a St. Louis marketing agency, was repositioning itself as future focused, and it needed its website to match. I designed a fully custom site for GBG’s that aligned with their revitalized identity. I also brought their new mascot CALLE to life.

130+ Responsive page designs

The team handed me the homepage and asked me to design the remaining 30+ pages for desktop and mobile, including services pages, thank you pages, forms and more.

2Breathing life into the site with CALLE

Our creative director wanted to avoid photography on the site, but with nothing in its place, the pages lacked emotion. Our solution was a mascot for GBG’s proprietary AI model, CALLE. Refined from three original concepts, CALLE shaped up as a lovable robot cat, easy to illustrate and animate across the site.

3Custom icons and graphics

To communicate GBG’s vast array of services, I created 25+ icons and abstract illustrations to populate the site. I was careful to maintain a similar visual style between elements.
