Murder Mystery Party

An exclusive invitation package for VIP guests

I partnered with friend and designer Tina to create a campaign promoting a Murder Mystery Party at the Lucky Blues Casino. We had to adapt our design for a variety of formats—a poster, t-shirts, an invitation, and a package to be mailed to the guest.

1Establishing a visual style with our poster design

My partner and I agreed to create our poster first to act as a foundation for our other elements. We settled on a playing card motif with custom suit icons that integrated traditional shapes with murder party props.

2Experimenting with format in our invitation design

Rather than inviting our guests a plain piece of paper, we sent a deck of cards. The entire invitation fits on the top card of the deck, and the box is blind embossed with our custom suit designs for a premium feel.

3Package design that excites

We knew none of our hard work would pay off if no one opened our box, so we enticed our guests with black blind embossing, custom type layouts, and illustrations.
